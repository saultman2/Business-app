Construction Business Manager App – Workflow Simplification & Crew Management Expansion

Use this prompt as a continuation of all previous development instructions. Do not remove existing functionality. Preserve all current features and continue improving them. The primary goal of this revision is to simplify the user experience while retaining enterprise-level capabilities behind the scenes.

Primary Objective

The application currently contains many powerful features. Instead of removing features, reorganize them into a simpler and more intuitive workflow.

A contractor should be able to open the app and perform the most common tasks within seconds.

The app should feel simple for a one-person business but scalable enough for larger companies with office staff, estimators, project managers, and field crews.

The overall goal is to create the easiest contractor management platform on the market while maintaining advanced functionality.

Final Goal

Transform the application into a contractor operating system that is simple enough for a contractor standing in a customer's driveway to generate a professional quote in minutes, while also being powerful enough to manage clients, crews, projects, finances, scheduling, invoicing, AI assistance, and business operations from a single platform. Every screen should have a clear purpose, minimal clutter, intuitive navigation, and premium visual design worthy of a paid SaaS product.
